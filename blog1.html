Top 5 Tips to Pass the CE Board Exam on Your First Try
By Engr. Clidez | June 24, 2025

Preparing for the Civil Engineering Board Exam can be overwhelming. But with the right mindset, strategy, and time management, success is well within reach. Here are my top tips based on my own journey and that of my students.

1. Master the Core Subjects

Focus on Structural, Geotechnical, Water Resources, and Transportation. These carry the most weight in the exam.

2. Make a Realistic Study Schedule

Don't overcommit. Block 3–5 hours daily with scheduled breaks. Include review center mock exams.

“Consistency will beat cramming every single time.”

3. Join a Study Group

  • Share review materials and techniques
  • Discuss challenging topics together
  • Keep each other accountable

4. Use Board-Like Practice Exams

Simulate the actual exam setting regularly. Use tools like the CE Board Exam Generator for randomized drills.

5. Take Care of Your Mental Health

Eat healthy, sleep well, and take breaks. Confidence during exam week stems from preparation *and* wellness.
